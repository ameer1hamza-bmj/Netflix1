Frequently Asked Questions

Netflix is a global streaming platform offering a vast library of movies, TV shows, and original content. It started as a DVD rental service in 1997 and became a pioneer in streaming in 2007.
Known for hit originals like Stranger Things and The Crown, Netflix has over 200 million subscribers worldwide, providing ad-free, on-demand entertainment across diverse genres..

Netflix offers flexible plans, from the affordable Mobile Plan to the Premium Plan with 4K streaming on four devices. Pricing varies by region; visit Netflix's website for details.

You can watch Netflix on any internet-connected device, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, laptops, and gaming consoles. Simply download the Netflix app or visit their website to stream.

You can cancel your Netflix subscription anytime by signing into your account, navigating to the account settings, and selecting "Cancel Membership." There are no cancellation fees, and you can restart whenever you want.

Netflix offers a vast library of content, including movies, TV shows, documentaries, anime, and originals across various genres. You can explore drama, comedy, action, and more, with something new added regularly.

Netflix offers a dedicated Kids profile with family-friendly shows and movies, ensuring a safe viewing experience for children. Parental controls allow you to set maturity ratings and restrict access to specific content.
